Achieving 100% Training Compliance

Through LMS and Attendance System Integration

Presented By:

Kokul S

Role:

Product Owner and Business Analyst

Objective

To improve compliance monitoring and eliminate manual verification challenges by integrating the Learning Management System (LMS) with the Attendance and Access Control System.

Presentation Duration: 8–10 Minutes

Business Challenge

Situation Before the Integration — compliance training verification was a fully manual, error-prone process

Existing Process (Manual)

  • Security guards maintained attendance records manually
  • Supervisors verified compliance through paper-based records
  • Training completion records were checked separately
  • Data reconciliation was done manually

Impact

The process was inefficient, error-prone, and heavily dependent on human intervention.

Key Challenges

  • Compliance rate averaged only around 60%
  • Manual verification consumed significant time daily
  • Frequent mismatches between training and attendance records
  • Difficulty identifying employees who missed training
  • Delayed reporting and lack of visibility
  • Increased risk of non-compliant personnel entering the workshop

Problem Analysis

Root Cause Analysis — three major issues identified

1. Disconnected Systems

The Learning Management System and Attendance System operated independently. There was no automated method to verify whether an employee had completed the required training.

2. Manual Verification

Security personnel and supervisors spent considerable effort checking records manually. This created delays and increased workload.

3. Lack of Real-Time Monitoring

Management could not track compliance status in real time. Non-compliance was often discovered only after manual reviews.

Conclusion: The absence of system integration was the primary reason behind low compliance and operational inefficiencies.

My Proposed Solution

LMS and Attendance System Integration

Whenever an employee failed to complete mandatory compliance training within the specified due date, the system automatically flagged the employee and restricted workshop access. Once training was completed, LMS data synchronized automatically, compliance status updated in real time, and workshop access was restored.

If training is incomplete past due date:

  • System automatically flags the employee
  • Workshop access is restricted automatically

Once training is completed:

  • LMS data synchronizes automatically
  • Compliance status updated in real time
  • Workshop access is restored automatically

Key Idea: Transform compliance verification from a manual process into an automated, real-time decision-making system.

Solution Workflow

End-to-End Automated Compliance Process
1

2

3

Result: No manual intervention required. Real-time compliance enforcement achieved.
